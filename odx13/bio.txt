Julia is a data scientist and programmer living in Montréal. In her free
time, she plays with open data at home, and organizes events for
programmers and aspiring programmers. She co-organizes Montréal All-Girl
Hack Night, a series of developer meetups for women, and PyLadies
Montréal, meetups and workshops for people to learn Python programming.
She is most interested in uses of data that make people's lives easier
on a day-to-day basis.
